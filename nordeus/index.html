This portfolio features some of my UI and UX work.

UNDP Selfemployment

I was contracted by the Macedonian company “GOVe” to work on a tablet-only Android application for UNDP’s Selfemployment project.

The application is meant to be used by UNDP employees to track the progress of applicants in the system through questionnaires and reports. Think of it as an upgrade from their current, established practices, using paper forms.

The application’s design needed to be clear and consistent.

My responisibilities were to design and develop the full front-end solution, as well as aid in the crafting of the back-end solution.

What follows are screenshots of one of the final versions of the product.

Some of the most challenging aspects were designing an interface which is clear and consistent, having in mind that the users do not have much experience in using tablets. This is why I decided to expose as much of the native Android patterns as possible, since this will not be the only application they will be using.
